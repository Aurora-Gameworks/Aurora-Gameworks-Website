Indie Games
Made by Gamers

Aurora Gameworks is a New Zealand based, Indie game studio. Our goal is to deliver creative, optimised, and fair gaming experiences. The Aurora team is made up of passionate developers who all have backgrounds of growing up close to gaming. We are active on YouTube, Facebook and Twitter, frequently posting updates on our projects. For those of you who are game developers, we also upload a range of tutorials on how to, to help you with your own game projects.

About

Aurora Gameworks started in 2018 by Andrew Welsh as a YouTube channel publishing tutorials about making video games in the Unreal Engine. Our first game was released on July 30 2019. Development of Library Tycoon began in [DATE].

During the nationwide lockdown of New Zealand, Alex Cutforth and Rodney Wessel Joined the team and began the development of Jurassic Smash.

Our Team

Andrew Welsh

Andrew Welsh is the founder and CEO of Aurora Gameworks, He is our Lead Model and Texture designer, and also does programming within the Unreal Engine.

Alex Cutforth

Alex Cutforth is the Lead Programmer of Aurora Gameworks. He specialises in programming C# within the Unity Engine. He also does website development In HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Rodney Wessel

Rodney Wessel is Lead Level designer and also does 3d modeling within Blender.
